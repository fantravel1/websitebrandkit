Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a branded emoji for my business?
Creating a branded emoji is simple: Choose an emoji that represents your brand's core value or industry, select 2-3 complementary brand colors, use our free generator to create all necessary assets (favicons, social images), and download your complete brand kit. The entire process takes under 5 minutes with no design skills required.
What sizes do I need for branded emoji assets?
For a complete branded emoji kit you need: Favicons in 16x16, 32x32, and 48x48 (ICO format), Apple Touch Icon at 180x180, Android Chrome Icons at 192x192 and 512x512, Open Graph Image at 1200x630 for Facebook/LinkedIn sharing, and Twitter Card at 1200x600. Our tool generates all these sizes automatically.
Can I legally use emojis as my brand logo?
Yes! Unicode emojis are free to use for commercial purposes. They're universal symbols designed for communication. The emoji designs may vary slightly between platforms (Apple, Google, Microsoft), but this actually ensures your brand emoji displays natively on every device.
What colors work best with branded emojis?
Choose colors based on your industry and color psychology: Blue (#0066FF) for trust and technology, Green (#10B981) for health and sustainability, Red (#EF4444) for energy and food, Purple (#8B5CF6) for creativity and luxury, Orange (#FF6B35) for friendliness and affordability. Use 2-3 complementary colors that create good contrast.

Yes, 100% free! Our branded emoji generator requires no payment, no sign-up, and no watermarks. You can create unlimited brand kits with all asset sizes included. Unlike custom logo design which can cost hundreds or thousands of dollars, branded emoji creation is completely accessible to everyone.
