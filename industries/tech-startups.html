Tech Startup Branding Tips

üí° Pro Tip: Keep It Simple

The best tech logos are instantly recognizable. A single emoji with a bold color works better than complex designs.

Color Psychology for Tech

  • Blue: Trust, reliability, professionalism (IBM, Facebook, Twitter)
  • Purple: Innovation, creativity, premium (Twitch, Yahoo)
  • Green: Growth, success, money (Spotify, Android)
  • Orange: Energy, enthusiasm, affordability (Amazon, Firefox)
  • Black/Dark: Sophistication, power, modern (Apple, Uber)

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Too many colors: Stick to 1-2 primary colors max
  2. Trendy emojis: Choose timeless symbols like rockets, not memes
  3. Low contrast: Ensure text is readable on your color
  4. Inconsistent usage: Use the same emoji+color everywhere
  5. Ignoring mobile: Test how your logo looks on small screens
